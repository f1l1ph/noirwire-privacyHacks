# Private Vault Use Cases

**Core concept:** Shared secret treasury where insiders see everything, outsiders see nothing.

---

## Institutional Finance

| Use Case | Description | Roles |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| **Hedge Fund Operations** | Fund managers execute trades privately — LPs can view performance but competitors can't see positions or strategy | Admin: Fund Manager, Member: Traders, Viewer: LPs |
| **OTC Trading Desks** | Large block trades without market impact — counterparties settle privately, no front-running | Admin: Desk Lead, Members: Traders + Counterparties |
| **Private Equity / VC Funds** | Capital calls and distributions without revealing fund size or portfolio to public | Admin: GP, Viewers: LPs |
| **Corporate Treasury** | Companies hold and move funds without revealing cash positions to competitors | Admin: CFO, Members: Treasury Team |
| **M&A Escrow** | Acquisition funds held privately until deal closes — both parties can verify balance | Admin: Escrow Agent, Viewers: Buyer + Seller |
| **Syndicated Loans** | Multiple lenders fund a private facility — borrower can't see individual lender positions | Admin: Lead Arranger, Members: Lenders |

## DAOs & Collectives

| Use Case | Description | Roles |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| **DAO Treasury** | Multi-sig treasury where members see balances but public doesn't know DAO's war chest | Admin: Council, Members: Core Contributors |
| **Grant Programs** | Grant committee privately allocates funds — recipients revealed only when they choose | Admin: Committee, Members: Reviewers |
| **Gaming Guilds** | Shared guild treasury for NFTs/tokens — members can see holdings, outsiders can't scout | Admin: Guild Leader, Members: Officers |
| **Creator Collectives** | Revenue sharing pool — creators see their cut, fans don't see individual earnings | Admin: Collective, Members: Creators |
| **Investment Clubs** | Friends pool capital privately — see each other's contributions, not visible to public | Admin: Organizer, Members: All Participants |

---

## Trading & DeFi

| Use Case | Description | Roles |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| **MEV-Resistant Trading** | Execute swaps without sandwich attacks — trade privately in PER, settle in batch | Members: Traders |
| **Private Market Making** | Market makers hide inventory and strategy — competitors can't copy or front-run | Admin: MM Lead, Members: MM Team |
| **Dark Pool** | Large orders matched privately — no market impact, no information leakage | Admin: Operator, Members: Institutional Traders |
| **Stealth Payments** | Pay contractors/employees without revealing amounts on-chain | Admin: Employer, Members: Payees |
| **Private Lending** | Borrow/lend without revealing your position size or collateral ratio | Admin: Protocol, Members: Lenders + Borrowers |

---

## High-Stakes & Sensitive

| Use Case | Description | Roles |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| **Whistleblower Funds** | Anonymous donations to whistleblower — donors hidden, recipient can withdraw | Admin: Trusted Org, Members: Recipient |
| **Journalist Protection** | Fund investigative journalism without revealing sources or backers | Admin: News Org, Members: Journalists |
| **Political Campaign Finance** | Private donations within legal limits — donors choose if/when to disclose | Admin: Campaign, Viewers: Compliance |
| **Family Office / Trust** | Generational wealth management — beneficiaries see their allocation, not total estate | Admin: Trustee, Viewers: Beneficiaries |
| **Divorce Settlements** | Assets held in escrow during proceedings — both parties verify without public exposure | Admin: Lawyer/Mediator, Viewers: Both Parties |

## Gaming & Entertainment

| Use Case | Description | Roles |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| **Esports Prize Pools** | Tournament winnings distributed privately — prevent targeted attacks on winners | Admin: Tournament Org, Members: Winners |
| **Poker/Casino Bankrolls** | Staked players share bankroll privately — backers see performance, others don't | Admin: Main Backer, Viewers: Stakeholders |
| **NFT Collector Vaults** | Whale collectors hide holdings — prevent copy-trading or targeted scams | Admin: Collector, Viewers: Trusted Advisors |
| **Prediction Markets** | Place large bets without moving the line — settle privately | Members: Bettors |

## Enterprise & B2B

| Use Case | Description | Roles |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| **Supply Chain Payments** | Pay suppliers without competitors knowing your vendor relationships or pricing | Admin: Procurement, Members: Suppliers |
| **Intercompany Transfers** | Move funds between subsidiaries without revealing corporate structure | Admin: Parent Co, Members: Subsidiaries |
